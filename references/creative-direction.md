# Creative Direction Reference

## Genre Visual Language

| Genre | Palette | Lighting | Locations | Camera Style |
|-------|---------|----------|-----------|-------------|
| **Pop** | Bright, saturated, clean | Even, polished | Fashion-forward, varied | Steady, smooth tracking |
| **Hip-hop** | High-contrast, neon accents | Hard shadows, rim light | Urban, nightlife, studio | Low angles, slow-mo |
| **Rock** | Desaturated, earthy | Harsh, practical | Warehouses, stages, outdoors | Handheld feel, fast cuts |
| **EDM** | Neon, hyper-saturated | Strobes, colored gels | Clubs, festivals, abstract | Dynamic, zooms, glitch |
| **R&B** | Warm amber, rich tones | Soft backlight, warm | Intimate interiors, golden hour | Shallow DOF, close-ups |
| **Indie** | Muted, faded, vintage | Natural, overcast | Nature, small towns, bedrooms | Static, contemplative |
| **Country** | Golden, warm earth | Golden hour, natural | Open fields, porches, roads | Wide establishing shots |

## Shot Composition Rules

Include in every SEEDREAM prompt:

**Composition** — specify one per scene:
- "rule of thirds, subject at left intersection"
- "centered symmetrical composition"
- "foreground framing element (doorway, window, branches)"
- "leading lines converging on subject"
- "negative space on left, subject at right third"

**Lens** — determines depth and feel:
- `35mm wide angle` — environmental, shows context, slight distortion
- `50mm standard` — natural perspective, versatile
- `85mm portrait lens` — shallow DOF, flattering, intimate
- `anamorphic widescreen` — cinematic, oval bokeh, lens flares

**Camera system tokens** — add cinematic texture:
- "shot on ARRI ALEXA, Kodak Vision3 250D film stock" (warm, cinematic)
- "shot on RED Komodo, natural color science" (clean, modern)
- "Fuji XT5, classic chrome film simulation" (vintage, muted)
- "shot on 16mm Bolex, film grain, soft focus" (indie, raw)

## Color Temperature as Storytelling

| Section | Temperature Shift | Emotional Signal |
|---------|------------------|-----------------|
| Verse 1 | Cool blues, desaturated | Isolation, longing, setup |
| Chorus | Warm amber, saturated | Connection, energy, release |
| Verse 2 | Slightly warmer than V1 | Growth, deepening |
| Bridge | Contrasting (opposite of chorus) | Doubt, tension, surprise |
| Final Chorus | Golden, most saturated | Resolution, triumph |
| Outro | Return toward cool, fading | Reflection, closure |

## Narrative Arc Patterns by Genre

**Pop**: "Ordinary → discovery → transformation → celebration"
**Hip-hop**: "Struggle → grind → rise → triumph"
**Rock**: "Frustration → rebellion → release → aftermath"
**EDM**: "Tension build → anticipation → drop → euphoria"
**R&B**: "Loneliness → attraction → intimacy → devotion"
**Indie**: "Observation → introspection → realization → acceptance"
**Country**: "Memory → present struggle → community/faith → peace"

## Mood-to-Lighting Map

| Mood | Lighting Description |
|------|---------------------|
| Hopeful | Golden light, open spaces, upward angles, warm tones, sunrise |
| Melancholic | Rain, overcast, cool blue, solitary figures, dusk |
| Energetic | Fast movement, bright colors, neon, dynamic angles |
| Intimate | Close-ups, shallow DOF, warm interior light, soft focus |
| Defiant | Low angles, harsh light, strong silhouettes, dramatic contrast |
| Nostalgic | Film grain, warm sepia, soft focus, vintage settings |
| Ethereal | Fog, lens flare, particles, pastel tones, water |
| Triumphant | Wide epic shots, golden hour, elevated vantage, sweeping vistas |
